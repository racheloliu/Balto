Balto

One of the goodest boys.

You may have heard of him because of the Disney movie, however, unlike the film, he was not a wolfdog. He was a Siberian Husky.

He was, however, a hero, who led a sledding mission across the Alaska territory to deliver vaccines to an isolated town.

Today, you can find a statue dedicated to Balto in New York City, NY, at Central Park.
